## 4. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

1. **Service Provision**: To provide healthcare diagnostic and prediction services
2. **Account Management**: To create and manage your user account
3. **Health Analysis**: To analyze symptoms and provide medical predictions
4. **Communication**: To send you notifications, reminders, and updates
5. **Legal Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations and regulations
6. **Research**: Anonymized health data may be used for medical research (with your consent)

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:
- **Explicit Consent** (Article 6(1)(a) and Article 9(2)(a) GDPR): For health data processing
- **Contract Performance** (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR): To provide our services
- **Legal Obligation** (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR): To comply with healthcare regulations
- **Legitimate Interests** (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): For service improvement and security

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- **Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM
- **HTTPS**: All data transmission is encrypted in transit
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Security Audits**: We regularly review and update our security measures

## 7. Data Retention

- **Active Users**: Data is retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Personal identifiable information is anonymized upon account deletion, while anonymized health data may be retained for research purposes
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

1. **Right of Access** (Article 15): Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Right to Rectification** (Article 16): Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
3. **Right to Erasure** (Article 17): Request deletion of your data
4. **Right to Restrict Processing** (Article 18): Limit how we use your data
5. **Right to Data Portability** (Article 20): Export your data in a machine-readable format
6. **Right to Object** (Article 21): Object to processing of your data
7. **Right to Withdraw Consent** (Article 7(3)): Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at [Your Contact Email].
